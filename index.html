    Rules

      Texas Hold'em

      5-Player Table • No Limit

      How to Play

      1. Each player receives 2 private cards (hole cards)

      2. Five community cards are dealt in the center

      3. Make the best 5-card hand using your cards + community cards

      4. Betting rounds: Pre-flop → Flop → Turn → River → Showdown

      5. Last player standing or best hand wins the pot!
